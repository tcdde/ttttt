About the Center

Supporting each learner to reach their full potential.

SALT (strategic alternative learning techniques) is a registered Non Governmental Organization with registration number 00NGO/R/1855, established with the aim of empowering young adults and children with developmental disabilities, basically Down syndrome, autism spectrum disorder, intellectual impairment, ADHD and cerebral palsy find vocational and life skills so they are able to live to their full potential and are able to better meet their diverse individual needs in their future.

The Center offers therapy, vocational education, life skills, entrepreneurship, counselling, advocacy and inclusive work opportunities.

Mission

To improve quality of life through vocational education, entrepreneurship, counselling, advocacy and advisory services.

Vision

To be a center of excellence providing quality services that enrich the lives of people with developmental disabilities.
